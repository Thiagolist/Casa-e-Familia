Sister

Good morning, sister,

How are the children? I have an immense desire to be close to those masterpieces of God’s will. I am sure Jesus has cared for you and strengthened you to teach your children with much love.

The teaching of ethics can explain the need for science, but science is not sufficient to explain the need for ethics. Fundamentally, for a statistician to be truly good, they must be ethical; otherwise their bias can directly influence the results.

There is something greater than knowledge to be taught to the children, and that is the story and the teachings of Christ.

May God preserve you all in His grace.
